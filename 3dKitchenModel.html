3D Kitchen Model

This model was the culmination of my final project for a computer graphics course. The goal was to integrate the year's learning on topics such as transformations, geometry, lighting, and texture properties into one cohesive design.

Continue reading for a detailed breakdown.

I designed an entryway scene leading into a kitchen, featuring wooden-textured counters and cabinets with marble countertops, a sink, and tile accents between the counters. The kitchen elements were made using cylinders and flat surfaces. To the left of the viewpoint is a table with two stool chairs, all created from cylinders, with straw-textured chair tops. The walls have an artsy texture and a screen door texture with a mock outside view. The floor is light wood, and the scene includes three light sources: one simulating indoor lighting, and two representing day and night light coming from outside. All colors, textures, and lighting were chosen to complement the scene's theme.
